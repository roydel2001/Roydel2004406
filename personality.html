My Strongest Trait: Overthinking

My tendency to overthink impacts my academic life in both positive and negative ways:

  • ✅ Positive: I analyze problems deeply, ensuring my coding projects are error-free and optimized.
  • ❌ Negative: I sometimes spend too much time second-guessing decisions, like debating the best network security approach for hours.
  • ⚖️ Balance: I’ve learned to channel this trait into thorough research while setting time limits to avoid burnout.

My Hero: Cristiano Ronaldo

“I never tried to hide the fact that my only goal is to be the best.”
— Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is my hero because he proves talent alone isn’t enough—you must outwork everyone. Growing up in poverty in Madeira, he transformed himself into a football legend through relentless discipline. His mindset inspires me to:

Personality Test Result

The Keirsey test identified me as a Logician (INTP). This explains my analytical nature and tendency to overthink systems. While the test accurately describes my problem-solving approach, I recognize that real-world cybersecurity requires balancing analysis with timely action.
